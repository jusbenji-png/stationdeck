How to back up your data (3 steps)

  1. Copy the path below — that is your StationDeck data folder.
  2. Open File Explorer (Windows key + E), paste the path into the address bar, and press Enter.
  3. Copy the entire data folder and paste it onto your USB drive or into your Google Drive / OneDrive folder. Do this once a month — label the folder with the date, e.g. data_backup_2026-05.
